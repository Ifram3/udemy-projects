The Best Movies According to Ifram3

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Prison Break

I enjoyed "Prison Break" series for its gripping plot twists and adrenaline-pumping escape strategies that kept me on the edge of my seat.

Money Heist

I loved "Money Heist" for its compelling characters and their personal struggles intertwined with the high-stakes heist, which created a unique and emotionally engaging narrative that resonated with me.

Narcos

I was hooked on "Narcos" for its gritty depiction of power struggles and the relentless pursuit of justice, as it weaves a narrative that resonates with my fascination for complex characters and real-life crime dramas.
